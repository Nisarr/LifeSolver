# 🌌 LifeSolver: Your AI-Powered Personal Operating System

**Built for the [Friction Hackathon](https://friction-hackathon.example.com) 🚀**

LifeSolver is a comprehensive personal management platform designed to streamline your productivity, finances, and habits into a single, intelligent interface. It serves as the core ecosystem for the **LifeSolver Extension**, providing the data and authentication required for a fully integrated "Growth Hacker" experience.

---

### 📥 Getting Started
1. Visit **[life-solver.vercel.app](https://life-solver.vercel.app)**.
2. **Create an account** (Required to sync with the extension).
3. Start managing your tasks, habits, and budgets.
4. Install the **[Supportive Browser Extension](https://github.com/Adnanshahria/Lifesolver-Extension)** to bring these goals into your browsing experience.

---

### ✨ Core Features (Main Website)

#### 📊 1. Intelligent AI Dashboard
- **Bento Hub:** A modern, high-performance overview of your entire life.
- **Daily AI Briefing:** Personalized summaries and actionable tips generated by Orbit AI based on your real-time data.
- **Activity Pulse:** Visual tracking of task completion, habit consistency, and study progress.

#### 📝 2. Master Task Management
- **Prioritization:** Organize tasks by urgency (High, Medium, Low).
- **Status Tracking:** Seamlessly move tasks from "Pending" to "Done."
- **Deadlines:** Integrated due-date management to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

#### 🔄 3. habit architecture
- **Consistency Tracker:** Log daily habits and visualize your streaks.
- **Analytics:** Understand your completion rates and build long-term discipline.

#### 💰 4. Integrated Finance Suite
- **Cash Flow Tracking:** Log every income and expense with category tags.
- **Monthly Budgets:** Set targets and track remaining funds in real-time.
- **Savings Goals:** Define financial milestones and track your progress toward them.

#### 🎓 5. Study & Inventory Management
- **Academic Tracking:** Manage subjects, chapters, and progress percentages.
- **Inventory System:** Keep track of your physical possessions and items in one place.

#### 🤖 6. Orbit AI Sidekick
- **Context-Aware Chat:** An intelligent assistant that understands your tasks, habits, and budgets.
- **Data Insights:** Ask Orbit AI for deep dives into your spending habits or productivity trends.

---

### 🧩 The LifeSolver Ecosystem
LifeSolver is a two-part system:
1. **The Web App (This Repository):** The "Brain" where you define your goals, manage your money, and set your schedule.
2. **[The Browser Extension](https://github.com/Adnanshahria/Lifesolver-Extension):** The "Enforcer" that adds psychological friction to distracting sites (like YouTube/Facebook) to keep you focused on the goals defined here.

